How to Automate Blog Writing with SEO Writing + Blogify

January 8, 2025 7 min read Akash Naik Content Creation, SEO

Content creation is the backbone of digital marketing, but producing consistent, high-quality, SEO-optimized blog posts can be time-consuming and expensive. What if you could automate 80% of your content creation process while maintaining quality and SEO effectiveness? In this comprehensive guide, we'll show you how to combine SEO Writing and Blogify to create a powerful content automation pipeline that produces publish-ready articles at scale.

The Content Creation Challenge

Modern businesses face an unprecedented content demand:

  • Volume Requirements: Companies need 4-6 blog posts per week to maintain relevance
  • SEO Complexity: Each post must target specific keywords and follow SEO best practices
  • Quality Standards: Content must be engaging, informative, and authentic
  • Cost Constraints: Professional writers charge $50-200+ per article
  • Time Pressure: Content calendars demand consistent publishing schedules

The solution? An automated content pipeline that leverages AI writing tools to handle the heavy lifting while maintaining human oversight for quality and brand consistency.

Step 1: Setting Up SEO Writing

SEO Writing is your content generation powerhouse. Here's how to set it up for maximum effectiveness:

Initial Configuration

  1. Account Setup: Sign up at SEO Writing and choose your plan based on content volume needs
  2. Keyword Research: Use built-in tools or import from Ahrefs, SEMrush, or Google Keyword Planner
  3. Brand Voice Training: Input examples of your existing content to train the AI on your style
  4. Content Templates: Create templates for different content types (how-to guides, listicles, reviews)

Optimizing SEO Writing Settings

Content Length Settings

  • Short-form: 500-800 words for quick tips and news
  • Medium-form: 1,200-1,500 words for standard blog posts
  • Long-form: 2,000-3,000 words for comprehensive guides

SEO Parameters

  • Primary Keywords: 1-2 main target keywords per article
  • Secondary Keywords: 3-5 related terms for topic coverage
  • Keyword Density: 1-2% for natural integration
  • Meta Descriptions: 150-160 characters with compelling CTAs

Common Pitfalls & How to Avoid Them

Over-Automation

Problem: Publishing AI content without human review

Solution: Always maintain human oversight and quality control

Generic Content

Problem: AI content sounds robotic and lacks personality

Solution: Add personal anecdotes, unique insights, and brand voice

Keyword Stuffing

Problem: Overusing keywords for SEO at the expense of readability

Solution: Focus on natural language and user intent

Neglecting Analytics

Problem: Not tracking content performance and optimization opportunities

Solution: Regular performance reviews and content strategy adjustments
